You're volunteering with [Raise the Voices](https://raisethevoices.org) —
advocacy for people facing enforced disappearance, arbitrary detention, and
other situations where they can't turn to their own government for
protection.

This guide gets you up to speed through a lot of little tasks. The idea is to
get you warmed up and talking to the rest of the team, not to bury you in
reading. Every page works the same way:

1. Read the short instructions.
2. Do the task in the real tool — there's always a link out.
3. Come back and check it off.

The menu on the left is the whole guide. You can always see where you are,
what's ahead, and jump anywhere — no getting lost down a rabbit hole. Do the
sections roughly in order: **Start here**, then **Warm-up tasks**, and on
down.

When you've worked through the checklist, you get a certificate — see
[Your certificate](/finish/certificate.md).
