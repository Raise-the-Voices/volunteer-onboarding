# Share a story

One post shared to your own feed puts a real story in front of people who'd
never otherwise see it. It's the smallest real thing you can do here — and it
still counts.

**Goal:** share one story or ally post that advances a cause you care about.

- [ ] Find one post worth amplifying — from Raise the Voices, from an ally (see [Follow our allies](/warmup/follow-allies.md)), or a credible story about the **Disappeared**, **Gaza**, **Sudan**, or an issue of your own
- [ ] Check it's from a source you trust before you boost it — we only amplify what's accurate and safe to share
- [ ] Repost or share it, and add one honest line of your own about why it matters
- [ ] Drop the link in the volunteer Slack (**#general** or a country/area channel) so others can amplify it too

**Done when:** you've shared one post and posted the link for the team.

> This is **step 8 of how we reach out — amplify** (see [Write and share a piece](/outreach/write-and-share.md)). And **Forgiveness, not permission**: you don't need sign-off to share a true story in good faith.
