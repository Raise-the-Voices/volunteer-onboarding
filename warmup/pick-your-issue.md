# Pick your issue

**Goal:** pick something that matters to *you* and write a short, one-paragraph brief.

The best volunteers work on what they genuinely care about. You can pick from
the issues Raise the Voices already works on, or bring one of your own.

- [ ] Choose an issue or region that pulls at you. A few we're active on:
  - **Disappeared & detained** people (by country)
  - **Gaza** — orgs and people on the ground
  - **Sudan**
  - or an issue you already care about from the [site tour](/start/tour-the-site.md) or the news
- [ ] Write **one paragraph**: what is happening, who it is happening to, and why it matters
- [ ] Share your paragraph in the volunteer Slack (**#general** is fine)

**Done when:** your brief is shared and you've read at least one other volunteer's brief.

> Tips: one paragraph, really. Facts with sources beat opinions. Keep what you
> wrote — you'll reuse it in the outreach, lobbying, and writing tasks.
