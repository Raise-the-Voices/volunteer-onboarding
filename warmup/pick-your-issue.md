# Pick your issue

**Goal:** pick an issue that interests you and write a one-paragraph brief.

- [ ] Look back at what caught your attention on [the site tour](/start/tour-the-site.md) — or browse the news for a human rights issue or case that matters to you
- [ ] Write one paragraph: what is happening, who it is happening to, and why it matters
- [ ] Share your paragraph in the volunteer chat

**Done when:** your brief is shared and you've read at least one other volunteer's brief.

> Tips: one paragraph, really. Facts with sources beat opinions. Keep what you
> wrote — you'll use it again in the outreach tasks.
