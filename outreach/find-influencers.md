# Find influencers

Much of our work is getting cases and issues in front of people who have
leverage — companies, public figures, journalists, officials.

**Goal:** build a short list of people who could influence one country or issue.

- [ ] Take the issue from [your brief](/warmup/pick-your-issue.md), or a case from [the cases database](/work/cases-database.md)
- [ ] Search: who has influence there? Example searches:
  - `companies investing in <country>`
  - `<country> biggest trading partners`
  - `celebrities from <country>` / `athletes from <country>`
  - `journalists covering <issue>`
- [ ] Pick three people or organizations from what you find
- [ ] Find each one on LinkedIn (or wherever they're publicly reachable)
- [ ] For each: note the name, their role, why they have leverage, and a profile link
- [ ] Share your list of three in the volunteer chat

**Done when:** your list of three is shared.
