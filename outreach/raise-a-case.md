# Raise a case with someone

**Goal:** bring one case or issue to one person who could help — as a question,
not an accusation.

We're not accusing anyone of anything — so we can't be accused of libel —
we're literally asking them to help with the case, because they have
leverage. A question gets read; an accusation gets deleted.

- [ ] Pick one person from [your influencer list](/outreach/find-influencers.md)
- [ ] Pick one case or issue — link to the case page or use your brief
- [ ] Draft a short message: who you are, the case in two sentences, and one clear question ("Are you aware of…?", "Could you help with…?")
- [ ] Share your draft in the volunteer Slack (**#general**) for feedback **before** sending
- [ ] Send it once you've gotten feedback
- [ ] Record that you reached out — *[FILL IN: where we track outreach — Slack thread / the task workspace / a sheet]*

**Done when:** the message is sent and the outreach is recorded.

> Most messages get no reply. That's normal — it still puts the case in front
> of them, and some do answer.
>
> Working a sensitive or unreported case? Don't post publicly about it before
> checking with your team — see [From practice to real cases](/work/active-cases.md).
