# Write and share a piece

Writing is one of our three core skills. A short, honest piece — a post, a
letter to the editor, an opinion piece — can put an issue in front of people who
would never see a news report. This is our step-by-step method.

**Goal:** write one short piece and share it.

- [ ] **1. Start from what you're connected to** — your own experience plus a little research. Reuse [your brief](/warmup/pick-your-issue.md).
- [ ] **2. Picture your audience** — like-minded peers? skeptical-but-open people? people overwhelmed by the news? Write for one of them.
- [ ] **3. Pick a format** — a public post, a blog post, a letter to the editor, or a guest opinion. Shorter is fine.
- [ ] **4. Find your thread** — the "vehicle" that carries the piece: a person to interview, a local group to spotlight, a book or film to react to, or a timely response to something in the news.
- [ ] **5. Draft it freely** — get it all down first, don't edit as you go.
- [ ] **6. Get feedback** — share the draft in the volunteer Slack. Reviewers: be kind and specific.
- [ ] **7. Publish** — post it, with relevant tags so the right people find it.
- [ ] **8. Amplify** — share the link across your platforms and in **#general**, and reply to anyone who comments.

**Done when:** your piece is published and shared with the team.

> Further reading: [Digital Outreach — Be the Story](https://impakter.com/digital-outreach-be-the-story/).
> And: you're done — take a second to enjoy that you gave your ideas life. ✨
