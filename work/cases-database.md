# The cases database

[cases.raisethevoices.org](https://cases.raisethevoices.org) is our database
of advocacy cases — the people we advocate for, reports about them over time,
and the actions taken on their behalf.

You don't need to master it now. This is a first look.

- [ ] Open [cases.raisethevoices.org](https://cases.raisethevoices.org)
- [ ] Browse the case list and open one case
- [ ] Read the person's story and the reports underneath it
- [ ] Find one case from a country you know something about

**Done when:** there's one case you could describe to someone else — who the
person is and what happened to them.

> Depending on which team you join, you may later learn to add reports and
> record casework here. Not yet.
